Data Deletion Instructions

If you have signed up for the hannah-web application using Facebook Login and would like to delete your data from our systems, you can do so in one of two ways:

Option 1: Remove App via Facebook

  1. Go to your Facebook account settings.
  2. Click on Apps and Websites.
  3. Find and select the app named hannah-web.
  4. Click on Remove.

This will revoke our access and automatically delete all data associated with your account from our servers.

Option 2: Manual Request

You may also send a data deletion request to our team by email at:
ssembatyadavid54@gmail.com

We will process your request and permanently delete your data within 7 business days.
